TITLE: Sleep Deprivation

OVERVIEW:
Lack of sleep is very common in students and affects both physical and mental health.

COMMON CAUSES:
- Late-night studying
- Excess screen time
- Anxiety about exams
- Poor sleep routine

COMMON SYMPTOMS:
- Daytime fatigue
- Irritability
- Headaches
- Poor concentration
- Low immunity

SELF-CARE:
- Maintain a consistent sleep schedule
- Avoid screens 1 hour before bed
- Avoid caffeine after evening
- Create a quiet sleep environment

RED FLAGS (SEEK HELP IF):
- Insomnia lasting more than 2 weeks
- Extreme daytime sleepiness
- Falling asleep unintentionally during activities
