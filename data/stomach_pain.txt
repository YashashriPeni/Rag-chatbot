TITLE: Stomach Pain

OVERVIEW:
Mild stomach pain is common in students and often related to diet or stress.

COMMON CAUSES:
- Skipping meals
- Eating junk or spicy food
- Stress and anxiety
- Gas or indigestion
- Food poisoning

STUDENT LIFESTYLE FACTORS:
- Irregular eating schedules
- Eating late at night
- Drinking excessive caffeine
- Exam-related stress

SELF-CARE:
- Drink warm water
- Eat light, simple meals
- Avoid oily or spicy foods temporarily
- Practice stress reduction techniques

RED FLAGS (SEEK MEDICAL HELP IF):
- Severe or worsening pain
- Persistent vomiting
- Blood in stool
- High fever
- Pain lasting more than 2–3 days
