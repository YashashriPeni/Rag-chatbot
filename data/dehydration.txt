TITLE: Dehydration

OVERVIEW:
Dehydration happens when the body does not have enough fluids.

COMMON CAUSES:
- Not drinking enough water
- Excessive caffeine intake
- Hot weather
- Skipping water during long study hours

COMMON SYMPTOMS:
- Headache
- Dizziness
- Dry mouth
- Fatigue
- Dark yellow urine

SELF-CARE:
- Drink 2–3 liters of water daily
- Reduce caffeine
- Eat water-rich fruits
- Take water breaks during study sessions

RED FLAGS (SEEK MEDICAL HELP IF):
- Severe dizziness
- Confusion
- Fainting
- Very low urination
