TITLE: Anxiety and Stress

OVERVIEW:
Stress and anxiety are very common among students, especially during exams.

COMMON CAUSES:
- Academic pressure
- Fear of failure
- Lack of sleep
- Social comparison
- Poor time management

COMMON SYMPTOMS:
- Rapid heartbeat
- Sweating
- Restlessness
- Trouble concentrating
- Irritability

SELF-CARE:
- Deep breathing exercises
- Short physical activity breaks
- Structured study plan
- Limiting screen time before sleep
- Talking to friends or mentors

RED FLAGS (SEEK HELP IF):
- Panic attacks
- Persistent sadness
- Thoughts of self-harm
- Inability to function normally
