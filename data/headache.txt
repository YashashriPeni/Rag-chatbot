TITLE: Headache

OVERVIEW:
Headaches are very common among students and are usually not serious.

COMMON CAUSES:
- Stress and exam pressure
- Lack of sleep
- Dehydration
- Skipping meals
- Long hours of screen exposure
- Poor posture while studying

STUDENT LIFESTYLE FACTORS:
- Studying for long hours without breaks
- Excess caffeine (coffee, energy drinks)
- Anxiety before exams
- Using phone or laptop late at night

SELF-CARE:
- Drink plenty of water
- Rest in a quiet and dark room
- Take short breaks from screens
- Maintain regular meals
- Sleep at least 7–8 hours

RED FLAGS (SEEK MEDICAL HELP IF):
- Sudden severe headache
- High fever with headache
- Vision problems
- Persistent vomiting
- Headache after head injury
